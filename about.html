About Us

Dzuvha Social Foundation is a non-Governmental organisation which was established in 2019.We saw a compelling need and growing problem of economical and educational gap between urban SA and rural SA with then decided to form an organisation to address such challenges using different solution.

Leadership

Our organisation is led by Mr Ndivhudzannyi Mabasa , he is a champion of social entrepreneurship and rural development, from the inception of this organisation Mr Mabasa

Mission

To integrate isolated rural population with mainstream economy, through education ,training and other development programs

Vission

The new epiphany of black African full of possibilities to uplift Africa.

Goals and Achivement

Our work is to provide opportunities to all Africans across SADAC region, especially the rural and peri urban.

Achivement

  • Connecting hundreds with potential employers.
  • Registering companies for young South Africans entrepreneur.
  • Offering extra classes to matriculants in various high schools around Thulamela municipality and Collins Chabane municipality.
  • We are closing the gap with 4IR training in rural areas

Goals 2022

  • Providing more for thousand grade 11 and 12 learners with Maths,Science,Accounting and English extra classes across South Africa.
  • Providing for rural region with 4IR training.
  • Other minor projects.
  • Provide training for entrepreneur across the country and setup funding network for them.
  • Climate change awareness.
